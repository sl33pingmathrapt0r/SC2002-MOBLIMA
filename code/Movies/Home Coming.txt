Home Coming
Rao Xiiaozhi
Home Coming is a 2022 Chinese drama film directed by Rao Xiaozhi and starring Zhang Yi, Wang Junkai and Yin Tao. The film picks up the story of two unarmed Chinese diplomats delving into a rebel force-controlled area in the war-wrecked Republic of Numea to lead 125 Chinese citizens safely back to China.
